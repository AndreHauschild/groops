SimulateStarCameraGnss

This program simulates star camera measurements at each satellite position. The resulting rotation matrices rotate from body frame to inertial frame.

The nominal orientation is simulated to be z-axis towards the center of Earth, y-axis is along solar panel axis, and x-axis forms a right hand system (points roughly towards the sun).

GPS attitude model: Kouba (2009), 'A simplified yaw-attitude model for eclipsing GPS satellites' https://doi.org/10.1007/s10291-008-0092-1

Galileo attitude model: https://www.gsc-europa.eu/support-to-developers/galileo-satellite-metadata

See also GnssAntex2AntennaDefinition and GnssYawBias2Instrument.
